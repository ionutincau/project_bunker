AUDIO FILES NEEDED (OPTIONAL)
==================================================

The following audio files should be placed in this directory:

  - bass_hum.ogg - Low, throbbing bass sound
  - glitch.ogg - Digital glitch sound effect
  - smash.ogg - Panel smashing sound
  - distortion.ogg - Audio distortion
  - glass_crack.ogg - Glass cracking
  - door_grind.ogg - Heavy doors grinding
  - horror.ogg - Horror sting
  - wire_rip.ogg - Electrical wire ripping
  - lullaby.ogg - Calm lullaby music
  - desync.ogg - Desync effect sound
  - massive_thrum.ogg - Deep, bone-shaking sound

==================================================
Note: If audio files are missing, the game will still run.
Ren'Py will simply skip playing the missing sounds.

Audio format: OGG Vorbis is recommended for Ren'Py games.
You can convert MP3/WAV to OGG using tools like:
- Audacity (free audio editor)
- FFmpeg command line tool
- Online converters

Free sound effects resources:
- freesound.org
- zapsplat.com
- soundbible.com
